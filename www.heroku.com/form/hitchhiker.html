A Hitchhiker’s Guide

A Whirlwind Tour of the Heroku Platform and Features

New to Heroku? Then join us as we explore the Heroku developer experience, designed to help you build, deploy and manage everything from large complex web applications to last night’s hack.

We’ll begin with a tour of Heroku’s many deployment options, demonstrate a handful of add-ons, run & share reports against live Postgres data with Heroku Dataclips, and review the runtime characteristics of a production application using app-level metrics. The last 25 minutes are reserved for your questions -- we'll have Heroku technical experts on hand ready to answer as many questions as they can.
