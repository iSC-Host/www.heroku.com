Heroku Commercial Sales Director

Heroku Commercial Sales Director - World Wide 

Heroku, along with its parent company Salesforce has transformed cloud computing with a revolutionary portfolio of Platform technologies.  And now we’ve got our sights set on the next generation of amazing apps!
 


Role Description


Heroku is hiring a Sales Leader with a proven track record of exceeding quota in Technology & Platform sales. You will focus exclusively on the Cloud application platform, Heroku and will be leading a team of Commercial Account Executives across the the globe -- covering Europe, Japan and the U.S.  Working in partnership with existing sales leadership, you will be driving revenue growth by penetrating the current customer base, and developing new customers in the region. This is a quota carrying role for the Heroku Platform product sales team across the globe.  



Your Impact:
Effective partnering with Salesforce and Platform leadership is mission critical.

  • To drive customer development and revenue across all Commercial accounts globally
  • Paticipate and lead client and prospect meetings or enage other corporate resources as necessary
  • Forecasting sales activity and revenue
  • Developing, executing and refining successful commercial sales processes
  • To leverage available field and marketing resources to drive events, campaigns and pipeline generation
  • To thrive in a highly matrixed Sales organization
  • To leverage business from new & established relationships
  • To attract, hire, train and retain top enterprise sales talent



 
Required Skills
 
  • Minimum 10 years of experience with managing commercial technology sales teams
  • Track record of being a top producer in both Individual Contributor and Management capacities
  • Appetite for success and willing to work on a team to reach goals


 
Desired Skills

 
  • Background in selling technology and platform solutions such as application development, database, business intelligence, integration.
  • Demonstrated ability to sell in a high volume, high velocity sales model.
  • Strong knowledge of the competitive products, solutions and sales tactics.
  • Consistent overachievement of goals.
  • Good written, verbal, and presentation skills.
  • Be creative with strong problem solving skills and the ability to succeed in a fast paced environment.
  • Proven ability to lead and work well as part of an extended sales team.
  • Experience in selling enterprise applications, architecture, solution architecture, application development.
  • Implementation and technology management.

 
 



About Heroku

Heroku is a thriving start-up owned by Salesforce, based in SOMA, San Francisco. Our work environment is fun and flexible while also ruthlessly focused on technical excellence and customer success. Our growing group offers lots of opportunities for hyper talented, self- motivated, no-drama and no-nonsense individuals who share our belief that cloud architectures are dramatically changing web application development and deployment, and want to be part of the vanguard of this movement.
