Product Security Engineer

Heroku’s security team is responsible for the overall security of the Heroku platform and our infrastructure, as well as for compliance with established security policies. We’re looking for dedicated security engineers who understand platforms and their unique security challenges. 

The primary focus of this role is the security of our products and features. You’ll be responsible for providing our engineers with expert advice through every stage of the secure development life cycle. You’ll research and participate in designing secure platform architecture. You’ll guide best practices on features under development, and you’ll perform assessments of features once they’re completed. 

If you’re the right person for this job, you have deep empathy for engineers. We are not the department of "nope"- you understand that your role as a security engineer is one of harm reduction. In short, you’re excited to design safeties for our tantalizing array of foot-guns. You should have experience in application or product security, particularly in threat modeling and web application testing.

Key responsibilities
  • Contribute across multiple engineering teams on security features
  • Research solutions and provide guidance to other engineering teams working in a variety of programming languages throughout the software development life cycle
  • Bring emerging security technologies to development teams and help to integrate with existing systems
  • Automate common or recurring security review tasks
  • Constantly question existing security practices and routines, and update, replace or automate them.

Key competencies
  • Scripting skills (our primary languages are Ruby and Go, but we’ll happily speak to candidates with other language backgrounds)
  • Web application development across multiple server-side languages and frameworks
  • Enthusiastic and quick learning of complex and incompletely documented systems
  • Able to work collaboratively across diverse engineering teams and products to meet organizational security goals
  • Able to quickly understand and effectively analyze someone else’s code 

Technologies
The strongest candidates will have worked with some of these technologies, and excited to learn the rest:
  • Amazon Web Services - EC2, S3, EBS, ELB, etc.
  • Languages - one or more of: Ruby, Go, Python, JavaScript, Java, Shell, Websockets
  • Databases - PostgreSQL and/or NoSQL Databases such as Redis
  • Logging - Splunk, AWS Kinesis, ElasticSearch, Syslog
  • Security Tools - Burp, Nessus, nmap

Location
Heroku is headquartered in San Francisco, California. The security team is distributed, and this position is open to candidates in other locations given sufficient time zone overlap and remote work experience.
