Senior Manager, Corporate Communications

Senior Manager, Corporate Communications

 

Heroku’s mission is to make the work of building and deploying applications as seamless and elegant as possible. We want to remove every obstacle that slows down creative coders, to let them focus on the quality and innovation of their apps while leaving everything else to our service.  And we believe that by doing this, we’re radically changing the world of app development for the better.

 

We’re looking for an experienced and tech-savvy corporate communications professional to create and carry this message through PR, social, and corporate communications channels. The person in this position will own and drive all aspects of Heroku’s communications program including PR and media relations, social media, content, exec spokesperson management, and more, working closely with all facets of the business, from our highly technical product team to Salesforce PR/AR.  We are seek a hard working, strategic thinker with strong PR, communications, social and content creation skills. This person should have a solid background building and running integrated PR campaigns and delivering stellar results.

 

POSITION DETAILS:

  • Lead the Heroku corporate communications and PR strategy, working with senior leadership across the company; create and execute clear messaging and positioning that helps us grow

  • Build and manage the Heroku thought leadership strategy; identify and develop thought leaders within Heroku, securing quotes, crafting industry POVs relevant to key trends and curating contributed content, manage executive speaking opportunities and press events

  • Identify the right media targets (mainstream press, social media leaders, and influential bloggers) for the app developer and cloud platform spaces, and building strong working relationships with relevant press to drive between-launches coverage on influential properties

  • Develop and manage the customer press program, onboard customers (in tandem with customer stories lead) with an ear to crafting media pitches and joint press opportunities

  • Work closely with product marketing, Heroku executives, and Salesforce AR  to compose on-message, compelling content for analyst briefings

  • Work on every aspect of Heroku PR and communications, from product launches to narrative development to handling complex messaging issues and crises

  • Manage and develop spokesperson team, including spokesperson selection, training, certification, and coaching

  • Drive compelling content for media, including pitches for various announcements/stories and press releases as well as contributed articles, FAQs and other materials

  • Work cross-functionally to uncover inspiring and differentiating stories for pitching and content development; generate and execute on content ideas for blogs and other channels that put us into the right conversations

EXPERIENCE/SKILLS

  • BA/BS in Communications, Journalism or Public Relations preferred

  • 5+ years of experience in public relations, spanning both agency and corporate in-house

  • Knowledgeable and experienced with cloud platforms and developer technology

  • Proven storyteller with excellent narrative instincts  

  • Ability to multi-task and quickly prioritize projects and communicate priorities to stakeholders

  • Ability to thrive under tight deadlines with limited resources

  • Excellent communication, writing, editing, and project management skills

  • Team player, capable of working well across a range of roles from marketing and sales to product and engineering
